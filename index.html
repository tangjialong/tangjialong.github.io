Jialong Tang (唐家龙)

I'm working at Alibaba Tongyi in Hangzhou now. I received my PhD degree from the Chinese Information Processing Laboratory at the Institute of Software, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2023, under the supervision of Professor Le Sun and Xianpei Han. I received my Bachelor's degree from Xiamen University in 2018, under the supervision of Professor Jinsong Su. Up to now, I have published several papers in the world's top conferences/journals such as ACL, EMNLP, AAAI and AIJ. My research interests include:

  • Natural Language Processing; Event Understanding
  • Qwen；Large Language Models; Multilingual
